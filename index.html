News Type

NATIONAL

National news refers to news stories and events that are of significance and relevance to an entire nation or country.
These news stories typically cover a wide range of topics, including politics, government, economics, social issues, culture, and events that have a broad impact on the country as a whole.

INTERNATIONAL

International news refers to news stories and events that are of global significance and relevance.
These news stories typically cover events, developments, and issues that transcend national borders and impact countries and people around the world.
